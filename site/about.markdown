<div class="text-center">
    <h4 class="about-subtitle">What We Do</h4>
    <p class="about-text">
        Psychotherapy of Litchfield is a private outpatient psychotherapy group practice serving high-functioning adults, couples, families, and youth. Our work is grounded in discretion, clinical excellence, and a deeply personalized approach to care.
    </p>
    <p class="about-text">
        Within a confidential and supportive setting, clients engage in thoughtful, collaborative psychotherapy that fosters insight, emotional clarity, and meaningful change. We help clients identify unhelpful patterns, broaden perspective, and build resilience while supporting healthier relationships and a more balanced, fulfilling life.
    </p>
    <h4 class="about-subtitle">How to Begin</h4>
    <p class="about-text">
        Beginning therapy is a personal and intentional decision that reflects a commitment to your well-being. At Psychotherapy of Litchfield, new clients are welcomed with an initial consultation with Dr. Sanchez for thoughtful therapist matching, or have the option to schedule directly with a preferred provider. This personalized approach allows us to carefully consider your goals, needs, and preferences, ensuring an exceptional therapeutic fit from the start.
    </p>
